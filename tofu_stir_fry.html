Tofu Stir Fry

Return to Index

Description

Tofu and Vegetable Stir-Fry is a quick, easy and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz. firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving
  1. Cut the tofu into 1-inch cubes and toss with the cornstarch until evenly coated.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the coated tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Add the bell peppers, onion, broccoli, garlic, and ginger to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  6. Add the cooked tofu back to the skillet and pour in the soy sauce mixture. Toss everything together until the sauce is evenly distributed.
  7. Season the stir-fry with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the stir-fry hot over cooked rice.
