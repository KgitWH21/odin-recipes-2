Ingredients

  • 8 oz. soba noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the soba noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  3. Add the sliced bell peppers, julienned carrot, snow peas, and sliced mushrooms to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet and stir-fry for another minute, or until fragrant.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and sesame oil to make the sauce.
  6. Add the cooked soba noodles and sauce to the skillet with the vegetables, tossing to coat everything evenly.
  7. Season the stir-fry with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the soba noodle stir-fry hot, garnished with chopped scallions or sesame seeds if desired.
